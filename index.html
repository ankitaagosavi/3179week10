Where are F1 Grands Prix Held?

Formula 1 Grands Prix have been held at every continent around the world except Antarctica!

Some of the oldest Grand Prix races have been held in Europe, with the Monaco Grand Prix being founded in 1929, and the British Grand Prix in Silverstone, which has been held since the first season of Formula 1 in 1950.

Driver Performances

Let's have a look at how these athletes have performed during the course of their careers!

One of the sport's most outstanding drivers includes Lewis Hamilton, who has picked up almost 200 podiums in his career! He is also only 1 of 6 drivers to have started more than 300 races in Formula 1.

His closest rival is Michael Schumacher, who has 155 podiums out of the 306 race starts that he has made.
